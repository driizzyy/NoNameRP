How Gangs Work

The gang system in NoName RP provides a structured way to engage in organized crime. Gangs can control territory, engage in criminal enterprises, and compete with rival organizations. Whether you're looking to join an established crew or start your own, the gang life offers unique roleplay opportunities and lucrative rewards.

Gang Features

Territory Control

Capture and defend territories across Los Santos. Control drug labs, chop shops, and hideouts to generate passive income.

Gang Stash

Shared storage for weapons, drugs, and stolen goods. Store items safely for gang operations.

Gang Vehicles

Custom gang vehicles with unique colors and upgrades. Show your colors on the streets.

Gang Wars

Engage in turf wars with rival gangs. Organized battles with rules and rewards.

Alliances

Form alliances with other gangs for joint operations and protection agreements.

Reputation System

Build your gang's reputation through criminal activity and successful operations.

Gang Hierarchy

Leader / Boss

Full control over gang operations, finances, and members. Can declare war, form alliances, and manage territory.

Full Gang Control Bank Access Recruit/Kick Members

Underboss

Second-in-command. Manages day-to-day operations and leads in the boss's absence.

Manage Operations Limited Bank Access Lead Missions

Lieutenant

Trusted senior member. Leads crews and organizes criminal activities.

Lead Crews Access Stash Organize Jobs

Soldier

Full-fledged gang member. Participates in all gang activities and operations.

Gang Activities Wear Colors Stash Access

Prospect

Trial member proving their worth. Limited privileges until earning full membership.

Limited Access No Stash Rights Must Prove Loyalty

Gang Rules & Guidelines

Initiation Required

All new members must go through proper roleplay initiation. No instant memberships.

No Meta Gaming

Gang communications must be IC. Use in-game phone or radio, not Discord voice.

Fair Gang Wars

Follow server rules for gang wars. Must have equal numbers and proper RP initiation.

Respect Territories

Don't sell drugs or do crime in rival gang territory without expecting consequences.

Cooldowns Apply

Gang activities have cooldowns. Can't hit the same gang multiple times in short period.

Safe Zones

No gang activity in safe zones like hospital, PD parking, or Legion Square.
