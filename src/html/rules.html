Server Rules

Read and understand all rules before playing

Welcome to NoNameRP

Our rules are designed to ensure a fair, immersive, and enjoyable roleplay experience for everyone. Failure to follow these rules may result in warnings, kicks, or permanent bans.

General Rules

1.1

Respect Everyone

Treat all players, staff, and community members with respect. No racism, sexism, homophobia, or discriminatory behavior of any kind.

1.2

No Metagaming

Using out-of-character information in-character is strictly prohibited. This includes Discord chats, streams, or any external communication.

1.3

No Powergaming

Forcing actions or roleplay on others without giving them a chance to respond is not allowed.

1.4

Stay In Character

Always remain in character unless absolutely necessary. Use /ooc for out-of-character communication sparingly.

1.5

Value Your Life

Fear RP is mandatory. If someone has a weapon pointed at you, comply reasonably. Value your character's life as you would your own.

Criminal Rules

2.1

Initiation Required

All criminal activities must be properly initiated with clear verbal communication before engaging in violence.

2.2

No RDM/VDM

Random Deathmatch and Vehicle Deathmatch are strictly prohibited. Every action must have valid RP reasoning.

2.3

Heist Cooldowns

Major heists have cooldown periods. Banks: 2 hours, Jewelry: 1 hour, Fleecas: 45 minutes.

2.4

Gang Wars

Gang wars must be approved by staff. Follow server procedures for territory control and gang conflicts.

Police/EMS Rules

3.1

Professional Conduct

All government employees must maintain professional behavior and follow department SOPs at all times.

3.2

Corruption Limits

Police corruption must be approved by high command and remain within reasonable limits.

3.3

EMS Neutrality

EMS must remain neutral in all situations. No picking sides, treat everyone equally.

Quality RP Standards

4.1

Character Development

Create realistic characters with backstories and proper development. No "superheroes" or invincible characters.

4.2

Realistic Actions

All actions must be realistic. Consider injuries, stamina, and physical limitations.

4.3

Voice Communication

A working microphone is required. Text RP is not permitted except in specific approved situations.

Enforcement & Appeals

Staff decisions are final. If you believe a punishment was unjust, you may appeal in our Discord server. Breaking rules will result in warnings, kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.
